🩸 Menstrual Phase

During your period, your iron and energy levels drop. Warm, comforting foods and iron-rich meals help replenish your body and soothe cramps.

🌙 Luteal Phase

Energy may dip as PMS approaches. Eat complex carbs, magnesium-rich foods, and B vitamins to stabilize your mood and cravings.

🍠 Sweet Potato (Complex Carbs) 🍌 Bananas (Potassium) 🥣 Oatmeal & Quinoa 🥥 Coconut 🌰 Walnuts & Sesame Seeds
